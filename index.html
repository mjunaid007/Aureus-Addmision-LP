Got Questions? We’ve Got Answers

What programs does Aureus University offer?

4-Year MD Program: For students with a previous one-year completion of Bachelor's degree or the completion of the pre-med coursework required.

5-Year MD Program For graduates from high school who need to have another year of basic sciences to progress to the regular medical curriculum.

How does Aureus support its students financially?

Scholarships: We offer diverse scholarships based on merit and financial need.

Flexible Payment Plans: Students have access to flexible payment options to ease the burden of tuition fees.

What are the living arrangements for students?

On-Campus Housing: Available and subject to space, newly admitted students have the opportunity to live in on-campus dormitories.

Off-Campus Housing: Several housing opportunities exist within the local community, including private houses, apartments, and rooms, all conveniently located near campus.

How can international students apply?

Application Guidance: Our admissions office also assists international applicants to the fullest and offers them visa processes and travel arrangements.

Cultural Integration: We provide programs and events that will enable international students to adjust to life in Aruba and life at Aureus.

What facilities does Aureus offer on campus?

State-of-the-Art Laboratories: Equipped with the latest technology for comprehensive, hands-on learning experiences.

Modern Library with E-Resources: Our expansive library houses over 50,000 books, articles, and journals, physically and digitally available to support our students' academic research and learning needs.
